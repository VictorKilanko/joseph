Why Mentor

More than giving back

Mentoring on Henceward is intentional, flexible, and genuinely rewarding — for you as much as for the person you guide.

🎯
Shape the Next Generation

The young people on Henceward aren't looking for a transaction — they're looking for direction. Your story, your failures, your wins are exactly what they need to hear. One conversation can change a life's trajectory.

💰
Earn on Your Terms

Set your own session rate. Henceward takes 15% — you keep 85%. Volunteer your time when you want, charge when you don't. No pressure, no quotas. Your availability, your schedule, your rules.

🏆
Build Your Reputation

Founding Mentor badge. Featured spotlights on the platform. Speaking invitations to Henceward Talks and Build Weekends. Mentoring here is a visible investment in your personal brand and legacy.

🔗
Join a Movement

Henceward isn't a marketplace — it's a movement. You're not just listing your calendar. You're joining a community of professionals who believe the next generation deserves more than algorithms and luck.

The Process

Three steps from application to live

We keep it simple. No long bureaucratic process — just enough to make sure every match is the right one.

01
Apply

Fill out a short application — your background, expertise, why Henceward resonates with you, and your availability. Takes about 5 minutes.

~5 min
02
15-Min Call

A quick values-alignment conversation with the Henceward team. Not an interview — just making sure we're building something together, not just filling a slot.

~15 min
03
Profile Live

We build your mentor profile together — bio, expertise tags, availability, rate. Once approved, you're live and mentees can book their first session with you.

1–3 days
