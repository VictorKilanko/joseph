## Section E: Empathy

**8.** Identify a specific problem you want to address — something real, not abstract. Now describe the people who have that problem:

- Who are they?
- What does their daily experience of this problem actually look and feel like?
- What have they already tried?
- What do they wish existed that doesn't?

If you can't answer these questions in detail, you haven't spent enough time with the people who have the problem. What would it take to change that?
