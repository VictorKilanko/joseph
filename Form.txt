MAILCHIMP INTEGRATION (do this in Tally)
=====================================

After adding all fields:
1. Go to Integrations tab in Tally
2. Click Mailchimp → Connect
3. Select audience: Henceward
4. Map: Email field → EMAIL
5. Map: Full name → FNAME
6. Add tag: Henceward Mentors
7. Save

This will automatically add every applicant to your
Mailchimp list with the "Henceward Mentors" tag.
